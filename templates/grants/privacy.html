Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

At ScholarGrants, we take your privacy seriously. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as your name, username, email address, and mobile money phone number when you create an account or request a withdrawal.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to operate the platform, process your reading summaries, verify your identity, and disburse funds to your mobile money account.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Your passwords are encrypted, and your financial information is never shared with third parties.
