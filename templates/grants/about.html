About ScholarGrants

ScholarGrants is an EdTech-FinTech platform designed to solve two major problems at once: the lack of reading habits among students and the financial burden of university education.

Our Mission

We believe that reading is the foundation of success. By incentivizing students with real mobile money rewards for every book they read and summarize, we are building a generation of well-read, financially empowered scholars.

How We Verify Learning

Unlike other platforms that just track time spent on a page, ScholarGrants requires students to submit detailed, original summaries of their reading. Our academic team reviews these summaries to ensure genuine comprehension before approving any financial rewards.
